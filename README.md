# Weather

Gives you your weather based on your IP Address.

## Example

```
+---------------+---------+------+------------+----------+----------+
| city          | country | temp | feels_like | temp_min | temp_max |
+---------------+---------+------+------------+----------+----------+
| New York City | US      | 36   | 31         | 28       | 41       |
+---------------+---------+------+------------+----------+----------+
```
